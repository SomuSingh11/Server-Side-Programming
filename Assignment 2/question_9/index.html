About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the "Pink City" due to the distinctive color of its buildings. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is a planned city with wide streets, angular roads, and colorful bazaars.

The city is a blend of traditional Rajasthani and Mughal architecture, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan. Jaipur forms one corner of the golden triangle of Indian tourism, along with Delhi and Agra.

From magnificent palaces and forts to vibrant markets and delicious cuisine, Jaipur offers a unique experience to every traveler. The city is famous for its handicrafts, textiles, jewelry, and the warm hospitality of its people.

Whether you're interested in exploring historical monuments, shopping for traditional artifacts, or indulging in authentic Rajasthani cuisine, Jaipur has something for everyone.
